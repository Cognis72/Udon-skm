Our Placement Process

A comprehensive, step-by-step approach to ensure your successful overseas job placement.

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your career goals, skills, and preferences. Our experienced counselors will assess your qualifications and match them with suitable opportunities.

  • Career assessment and goal setting
  • Skills evaluation and gap analysis
  • Country and industry preference discussion
  • Documentation requirements overview
Duration: 1-2 weeks
2

Document Preparation

Our team assists you in preparing all necessary documents, ensuring they meet international standards and specific country requirements.

  • Resume/CV optimization for target market
  • Educational credential evaluation
  • Professional certification verification
  • Language proficiency test preparation
Duration: 2-4 weeks
3

Job Matching & Application

We match your profile with suitable job opportunities and handle the application process on your behalf, leveraging our network of international employers.

  • Job opportunity identification
  • Application submission to employers
  • Initial employer screening coordination
  • Interview scheduling and preparation
Duration: 3-6 weeks
4

Interview & Selection

We provide comprehensive interview preparation and support you through the selection process, including technical assessments and cultural fit evaluations.

  • Interview coaching and mock sessions
  • Technical skill assessment preparation
  • Cultural orientation and tips
  • Salary negotiation guidance
Duration: 2-4 weeks
5

Visa & Documentation

Once you receive a job offer, we guide you through the visa application process and ensure all legal requirements are met for your destination country.

  • Work visa application assistance
  • Medical examination coordination
  • Police clearance certificate guidance
  • Embassy appointment scheduling
Duration: 4-8 weeks
6

Pre-Departure Support

We provide comprehensive pre-departure orientation to ensure you're fully prepared for your new life and career abroad.

  • Cultural orientation sessions
  • Accommodation assistance
  • Banking and financial setup guidance
  • Travel arrangements support
Duration: 1-2 weeks
7

Post-Arrival Support

Our support doesn't end when you arrive. We provide ongoing assistance to ensure your smooth integration into your new workplace and community.

  • Airport pickup and initial orientation
  • First week settlement assistance
  • Workplace integration support
  • Ongoing consultation for first 6 months
Duration: Ongoing
