Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the placement process take?

The typical placement process takes 4-6 months from initial consultation to departure. This can vary depending on the specific job requirements and destination country.

What are the fees for your services?

Our consultation and initial assessment are completely free. Service fees are only applicable after successful job placement and are transparent with no hidden costs.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance including document preparation, application guidance, and embassy appointment coordination.

What support do you offer after arrival?

We provide 6 months of post-arrival support including accommodation assistance, cultural orientation, and workplace integration guidance.
